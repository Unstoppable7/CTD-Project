Hello team,

My name is Cristian Rosales and I am excited to present my project to you. This project is based on the Art Institute of Chicago API and offers a platform to explore artworks, artists and exhibitions in an intuitive and efficient way.

Upon entering the platform, users find three main sections: Artworks, Artists and Exhibitions. Each section features a list of cards representing corresponding items, such as paintings, sculptures, individual artists, or exhibitions.

A key feature of the platform is pagination capability, which allows users to explore a large number of elements without having to load the page excessively with data. Additionally, the navigation bar includes a search engine that allows users to search for specific items using search terms.

Each card in the item list includes a "Details" button that takes users to a dedicated page with additional information about the selected item. For example, in the details of a Artwork, users can find information about the artist or artists who created it. From there, they can directly access the details for that artist.

Similarly, in the details of an artist or an exhibition, a list of related artworks is displayed. This allows users to explore more works of art and delve deeper into the context and history behind each one.

In summary, my project offers a complete experience to explore and discover art through an intuitive and friendly interface. I'm excited to share it with you and I hope you enjoy it as much as I enjoyed creating it.
